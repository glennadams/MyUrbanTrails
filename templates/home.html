Welcome to The Urban Trail Planner

Design a route for walking in an urban setting. Determine estimated walking time and distance

How to get started

Step 1: Use the map tools to pan and zoom to a desired area

Step 2: Select the polyline tool tool from the toolbar

Step 3: Move pointer start location on the map

Step 4: Then click along a the desired route, following roads & trails

Step 5: At the end hover mouse over the blue dot and click

(Mapbox will draw an optimized route returning distance & time)

Step 6: Review the route.

Step 7: To delete route, select trash button tool from the toolbar

Step 8: Repeat Steps 1 thru 7 until you have the Urban Trail you want

Registering on this site allows:

  • Storing your designed routes
  • Managing stored routes
