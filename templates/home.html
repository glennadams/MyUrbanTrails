Welcome to The Urban Trail Planner

This site allows you to plot routes for walking in urban areas. You can design a route and determin estimated walking time and distance

Registering on this site allows:

  • Storing you designed routes
  • Recalling stored routes
  • Adding notes to a route
  • Store an address as a starting location

Designing a route

Use the map tools to pan and zoom to a desired area

The bottom two icons are the drawing tool and trash to delete a route

Activate the drawing tool. Then click along the disired route

At the end move the cursur over the last location. It will highlite.

Then click this location and it will generate an optimized route

Note the information window will display direction, distance and time

Use the trash button to delete the trail and create another
